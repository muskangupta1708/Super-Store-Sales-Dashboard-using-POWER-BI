#  Super Store Sales Dashboard & Sales Forecasting (Power BI)

##  Project Summary

Developed an **end-to-end sales analytics and forecasting solution** using Power BI to analyze retail performance and support data-driven decision making. The project combines **historical sales analysis** with a **15-day sales forecast**, delivering executive-level insights into revenue, profit, customer behavior, and regional performance.

---

## Business Problem

Retail stakeholders needed:

* Clear visibility into **sales and profit performance**
* Identification of **top regions, products, and customers**
* A **short-term sales forecast** to support inventory and operational planning

---

##  Solution

* Built **interactive Power BI dashboards** with drill-downs and filters
* Designed KPI cards to track **Sales, Profit, Orders, and Shipping Time**
* Implemented **time-series forecasting** for the next 15 days
* Enabled analysis by **region, category, segment, payment mode, and ship mode**

---

##  Key Results & Insights

* Analyzed **1.6M+ in sales** across **22K orders**
* Identified **West & East regions** as top revenue drivers
* Found **Office Supplies and Technology** as leading categories
* Determined **Consumer segment** contributes ~48% of total sales
* Forecast indicates **stable to increasing demand** in the near term
